treat as credible, closing the gap found 16 Jul 2026 (PrepStep absent from every

targets, sell ONLY the permanent free tier + 30-day trial (the teach-first lessons,

Two things about the offer:
- "Free account to look around" = they can just use the permanent free tier; if they
  want full access for a review, comp them via the admin panel once they say yes.
- No pitch commits you to anything until they reply.
