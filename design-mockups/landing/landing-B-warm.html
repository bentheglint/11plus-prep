Good questions

The things parents ask us.

What makes PrepStep different from other 11+ practice? +
Most tools test first and explain later, if at all. PrepStep teaches the method before every focused quiz, then keeps a patient tutor on hand for anything tricky. Your child learns how to do it, rather than guessing and hoping.
Is it right for the exam my child is sitting? +
PrepStep is built for the 11+ for grammar schools in the Bournemouth and Poole area, covering Maths, English and Verbal Reasoning. The practice is shaped around the kind of questions those exams ask.
Will the tutor just give my child the answers? +
No. While your child is still working on a question, the tutor will not reveal the answer. It nudges them with a smaller question to get them thinking. Only once they have committed to an answer does it explain, in plain, friendly words.
Do I need to be there to help? +
Not at all. The lessons do the teaching and the tutor handles the tricky moments. The parent dashboard lets you keep an eye on progress in a glance, so you can step back and let them get on with it.
Is it safe and private for my child? +
Yes. PrepStep is built for children, with a safe tutor, no advertising, and no data sold on. We handle personal data in line with UK GDPR. Your child's work is theirs and yours, and no one else's.
