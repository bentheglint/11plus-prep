## Overview

Pretest Plus is a specialist pay-per-resource provider targeting the independent school and selective school entrance market rather than the state grammar school 11+ mainstream. Its primary focus is ISEB Common Pre-Tests, CAT4 (Cognitive Abilities Test), Cambridge Select Insight, UKiset, and analogous cognitive and academic assessments used by independent schools at 11+, 10+, 13+, and beyond. It is not a direct competitor to grammar-school-focused apps but occupies an adjacent high-value niche where families are typically spending significant sums on private school fees and are willing to invest heavily in entrance preparation. There is no subscription model; all products are purchased individually or in bundles.

## Pricing tiers

Pretest Plus uses transactional pricing. Confirmed price points from previous agent research (direct fetch did not yield individual product pages):

| Product type | Typical price |
|---|---|
| Individual test (e.g. single ISEB practice test) | ~£49 |
| Skill module | ~£79 |
| Small bundles | From ~£184 |
| Comprehensive bundles | Up to ~£1,057 |
| School packages (institutional) | Custom pricing |

Discount codes observed:
- **PPDISCOUNT:** 10% off orders over £80; 20% off orders over £200
- **SPRING:** 30% off for 36 hours (seasonal; excludes some product types)

## Free vs paid

No free trial observed. No structured preview or sample period. The high per-item prices suggest the product is primarily sold through parent word-of-mouth and school recommendation rather than conversion-funnel trials.

## Family / sibling pricing

None observed. Given the niche, high-cost nature of the product, the majority of purchasers are likely single-child households targeting a specific school at a specific time.

## Unusual mechanics

- **Extremely high bundle ceiling (~£1,057):** This reflects the willingness of independent school families to invest in entrance preparation at a level that has no parallel in the grammar school 11+ market.
- **Performance analysis by age:** Products include benchmarking against peer groups, which is a meaningful differentiator for cognitively-focused assessments like CAT4.
- **School/institutional packages:** Pretest Plus sells to prep schools directly, not just individual families — a B2B revenue stream absent from most direct-to-consumer 11+ products.
- **14 product categories** covering tests from Year 3 entry (7+) through university admissions (TMUA), giving it unusual breadth within the selective admissions vertical.

## Positioning copy

- "Highly accurate practice resources" that replicate actual test formats, timing, and structure.
- "Performance analysis by age" with peer group benchmarking — emphasises competitive intelligence, not just practice volume.
- "Detailed answers and explanations for every question" — positions error analysis as central to improvement.

## Applicability to UK 11+ app pricing

Pretest Plus is not a direct competitive threat to a grammar school 11+ app but illustrates the ceiling of willingness-to-pay in the broader selective admissions market: over £1,000 per child for comprehensive independent school prep. If the app ever expands into independent school preparation or ISEB content, it enters this higher-spend segment where subscription pricing may need to shift upward to match perceived value and parent expectations.
