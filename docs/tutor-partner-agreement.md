## 1. What PrepStep provides

As a Partner Tutor you receive:

- Access to the PrepStep tutor dashboard to manage your pupils' 11+ practice
- A unique invite code to share with parents
- A dashboard showing each connected pupil's quiz scores, topic performance, and practice history
- The ability to set and track homework assignments through the platform

---

## 2. Commission

PrepStep will pay Partner Tutor **25% of net subscription revenue** from each pupil who:

(a) joined PrepStep using your invite code, **and**
(b) holds an active paid subscription at the time of payment

**"Net subscription revenue"** means the amount received by PrepStep after Stripe's payment processing fee (approximately 1.5% + 20p per transaction).

**Payment:** Commission is calculated monthly and paid by bank transfer within 14 days of the end of each calendar month. Minimum payout: £10 — amounts below this roll over to the next month.

**Duration:** This commission applies for as long as the pupil remains a paying subscriber, with no time limit or cap.

**If a pupil leaves and returns:** Commission stops on cancellation. If the pupil later resubscribes independently (not through your invite code), commission does not resume. If they rejoin via your invite code, commission resumes.

**If you remove a pupil:** If you remove a pupil from your roster, or the pupil disconnects from you, commission stops from that date.

---

## 3. Your obligations

As a Partner Tutor you agree to:

- Use the platform only for legitimate private tutoring of pupils actively on your roster
- Not share your login credentials with anyone
- Not use pupil data for any purpose other than supporting that pupil's 11+ preparation
- Keep all pupil data confidential — do not share names, scores, or progress with anyone other than that pupil's parent or guardian
- Notify PrepStep promptly at hello@prepstep.co.uk if you become aware of a data breach or any unauthorised access
- Comply with UK GDPR in your handling of any pupil personal data you access through the platform

---

## 4. Pupil data

The data you see through PrepStep (child's first name, quiz scores, topic performance, practice history, and parent's name) is provided solely to enable you to tutor that child effectively.

You must:

- Only access data for pupils actively on your roster
- Not export, copy, or retain pupil data outside the PrepStep platform beyond what is needed for an active tutoring session
- Delete any personal notes or records relating to a pupil within 30 days of the tutoring relationship ending
- Act as an independent data controller for your own tutoring purposes, and take responsibility for your own compliance with UK GDPR in your tutoring practice

PrepStep remains the data controller for data held on the platform. Parents can disconnect their child from you at any time, which will immediately remove your access to that child's data.

---

## 5. PrepStep's obligations

PrepStep will:

- Maintain the tutor dashboard in working order
- Calculate and pay commission accurately and on time
- Give you at least 30 days' written notice of any reduction in the commission rate or material changes to the platform
- Keep your personal details (name, email) confidential and not share them with third parties without your consent

---

## 6. Ending this agreement

Either party may end this agreement by giving 30 days' written notice to hello@prepstep.co.uk.

PrepStep may terminate immediately and without notice if you:

- Breach the data handling obligations in sections 3 or 4
- Use the platform in a way that harms PrepStep's users or reputation
- Provide false information when signing up as a tutor

**On termination:** your access to the platform is removed at the end of the notice period. Commission for any month already completed is paid on the normal schedule. No commission accrues after your access is removed.

---

## 7. No employment or agency

This agreement creates a commercial partnership only. You are not an employee, worker, agent, or representative of PrepStep. You are responsible for declaring and paying your own tax and National Insurance on any commission received.

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
