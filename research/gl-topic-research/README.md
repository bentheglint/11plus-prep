## Key Findings Across Topics

- **Circles** (pi, circumference, area) are NOT in KS2 curriculum -- unlikely on GL paper
- **Compound speed units** only "may be introduced" in Year 6 -- high-value prep topic
- **GL tests negative number multiplication/division** at D3, beyond Year 6 curriculum
- **Antonyms** didn't appear standalone in 8 scanned practice papers but remain in GL's type pool
- **Grammar accounts for ~39% of the English paper** across 3 sections (word class, error-spotting, cloze)
- **Part-to-whole vs part-to-part** is the single most exploited misconception in Ratio
