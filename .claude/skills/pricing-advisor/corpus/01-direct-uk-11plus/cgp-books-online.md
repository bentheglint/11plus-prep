## Overview

CGP Books Online is the digital extension of CGP's ubiquitous revision guide brand — the bright-yellow paperbacks found in virtually every UK primary school child's bedroom. The online platform offers unlimited 11+ practice across all subjects and test providers (GL, CEM, ISEB) under a single flat subscription, with no tier structure. It targets budget-conscious families who already trust the CGP brand from bookshop purchases. The platform's extreme price point — around £6/month — makes it the cheapest credible option in the direct UK 11+ market by a significant margin, and it relies almost entirely on brand recognition rather than feature depth to convert.

## Free vs paid

No free trial observed. CGP's brand familiarity (the books are already in the home for many families) reduces the need for a trial — parents know what they are buying. Sample content may be visible on the website, but no structured trial period has been confirmed.

## Family / sibling pricing

None observed. The single flat subscription may in practice serve multiple children in a household, though this is not explicitly marketed.

## Unusual mechanics

- **No tier complexity** — one product, one price. This is a deliberate simplicity play that removes any decision paralysis at the point of purchase.
- **Annual discount (~30%)** — meaningful saving that incentivises upfront commitment.
- The product relies almost entirely on the CGP brand halo; there is no meaningful marketing investment in feature differentiation or outcome claims.

## Positioning copy

CGP does not publish outcome statistics or placement claims. The implicit positioning is: "You already trust our books — now get the digital version." This is brand extension, not a ground-up digital product pitch.

## Applicability to UK 11+ app pricing

CGP defines the floor of the credible market: £6/month. A new entrant should not try to compete at this price point — CGP wins on brand alone. Instead, use CGP as the lower anchor in a good/better/best framing: "More than a worksheet generator, less than a tutor." Anything priced between ~£15–35/month has clear air above CGP and a meaningful value story to tell.
