PrepStep Tutor Terms

Version 1.0 — 8 June 2026

By creating a tutor profile on PrepStep, you agree to these terms. They cover the commercial arrangement (commission), your responsibilities with pupil data, and what happens if either party ends the relationship.

PrepStep is operated by Ben Jackson (sole trader), hello@prepstep.co.uk ("PrepStep" or "we"). The person creating a tutor profile is the "Partner Tutor" or "you".

1. What PrepStep provides

As a Partner Tutor you receive:

  • Access to the PrepStep tutor dashboard to manage your pupils' 11+ practice
  • A unique invite code and link to share with parents
  • A dashboard showing each connected pupil's quiz scores, topic performance, and practice history
  • The ability to set and track homework assignments through the platform

2. Commission

PrepStep will pay you 25% of net subscription revenue from each pupil who joined via your invite code and holds an active paid subscription.

Net subscription revenue means the amount received by PrepStep after Stripe's payment processing fee (approximately 1.5% + 20p per transaction).

Payment: Commission is calculated monthly and paid by bank transfer within 14 days of month end. Minimum payout: £10 — smaller amounts roll over.

Duration: Commission continues for as long as the pupil remains a paying subscriber, with no time limit or cap.

If a pupil leaves and returns: Commission stops on cancellation. It resumes only if they rejoin using your invite code. Independent resubscriptions do not attract commission.

If you remove a pupil: Commission stops from the date of removal.

3. Your obligations

As a Partner Tutor you agree to:

  • Use the platform only for legitimate private tutoring of pupils actively on your roster
  • Not share your login credentials with anyone
  • Not use pupil data for any purpose other than supporting that pupil's 11+ preparation
  • Keep all pupil data confidential — do not share names, scores, or progress with anyone other than that pupil's parent or guardian
  • Notify PrepStep promptly at hello@prepstep.co.uk if you become aware of a data breach or unauthorised access
  • Comply with UK GDPR in your handling of any pupil personal data you access through the platform

4. Pupil data

The data you see through PrepStep (child's first name, quiz scores, topic performance, practice history, and parent's name) is provided solely to enable you to tutor that child effectively.

You must:

  • Only access data for pupils actively on your roster
  • Not export or retain pupil data outside PrepStep beyond what is needed for an active tutoring session
  • Delete any personal notes or records relating to a pupil within 30 days of the tutoring relationship ending
  • Act as an independent data controller for your own tutoring purposes, and take responsibility for your own UK GDPR compliance

PrepStep remains the data controller for data held on the platform. Parents can disconnect their child from you at any time, immediately removing your access to that child's data.

5. PrepStack's obligations

PrepStep will:

  • Maintain the tutor dashboard in working order
  • Calculate and pay commission accurately and on time
  • Give you at least 30 days' written notice of any reduction in commission rate or material change to the platform
  • Keep your personal details confidential and not share them with third parties without your consent

6. Ending the arrangement

Either party may end this arrangement with 30 days' written notice to hello@prepstep.co.uk.

PrepStep may terminate immediately if you breach the data handling obligations in sections 3 or 4, use the platform in a way that harms PrepStep's users or reputation, or provide false information when signing up.

On termination: your platform access is removed at the end of the notice period. Commission already earned is paid on the normal schedule. No commission accrues after access is removed.

7. No employment or agency

This arrangement is a commercial partnership only. You are not an employee, worker, agent, or representative of PrepStep. You are responsible for declaring and paying your own tax and National Insurance on any commission received.

8. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of England and Wales.

9. Changes to these terms

If we make material changes to these terms, we will notify you by email at least 30 days before the changes take effect. Continued use of the platform after that date constitutes acceptance. If you do not agree, you may end the arrangement before the changes take effect.
