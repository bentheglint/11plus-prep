## Overview

Tassomai is a UK-built adaptive learning app that uses spaced repetition and low-stakes quizzing to embed core subject knowledge. Originally focused on GCSE revision, it has expanded downward to cover Years 3–11, making it relevant to the 11+ preparation market at its lower tier. For Years 3–6 specifically, it covers Maths, English, and Science (the latter from Year 5). The platform generates a personalised daily question set for each learner, targeting the topics they find hardest. Tassomai's core insight is that frequent retrieval practice (not passive reading) is the most effective revision method — a position backed by cognitive science literature. It positions as a smarter, more accessible alternative to private tutoring.

## Pricing tiers

| Year group tier | Monthly price | Subjects covered |
|---|---|---|
| Years 3–6 (incl. 11+ prep) | £8.99/month | Maths, English, Science (from Yr 5) |
| Years 7–8 | £29.99/month | Science, English, Maths, Latin, MFL vocab |
| Years 9–11 (GCSE) | £44.99/month | Science, English, Maths, Geography, History, Computer Science, Latin, MFL |

There is no published annual plan; pricing is month-to-month for all tiers. This is unusual in the UK edtech subscription market, where annual plans with a discount are the norm.

## Free vs paid

- A **7-day free trial** is available for families (referenced on the homepage; terms subject to change).
- No permanent free tier.
- After the trial, full monthly pricing applies with no annual alternative currently published.
- The absence of an annual plan at the Years 3–6 tier means the effective annual cost is £107.88 — higher than many comparable annual subscriptions in the market.

## Family / sibling pricing

A **10% sibling discount** applies automatically when a second or subsequent learner is added to a parent account. Discounts stack across all learners on the account. This is a modest incentive compared to flat family plans at competitors, but it is automatic and requires no coupon code or manual application.

## Schools licensing (if applicable)

Tassomai operates a separate schools product (primarily aimed at secondary schools for GCSE preparation). It is trusted by 500+ schools and positions the school tier as a curriculum-embedding tool for teachers rather than a pupil-self-study product. School pricing is not published publicly; schools request a quote. The school and family products are distinct — families buy direct.

## Unusual mechanics

- **GCSE grade guarantee (Years 9–11 tier only):** If a pupil completes 100% of the GCSE course on Tassomai, they are guaranteed a grade 7–9 (equivalent to A–A*). If not achieved, the full subscription is refunded. This guarantee applies only to the GCSE tier, not to the Years 3–6 / 11+ tier.
- **Retrieval science positioning:** Tassomai is unusually explicit about the cognitive science behind its approach, citing spaced repetition and low-stakes testing as the mechanism of action. This gives the product a credible "evidence-based" positioning.
- **No annual plan:** All tiers are monthly-only, which is unusual. This may reflect a desire to reduce friction at entry (no large upfront commitment) but sacrifices the LTV uplift that annual plans typically generate.
- **Parent dashboard:** Parents can monitor progress and identify subject-specific gaps across all learners on a single account.
- **#1 EdTech50 2025:** External validation used prominently in marketing.

## Applicability to UK 11+ app pricing

Tassomai's £8.99/month (no annual option) for the Years 3–6 tier establishes a lower-end price anchor for 11+ prep specifically. Its grade guarantee model — though scoped to GCSE at present — is directly transferable to 11+ as a concept: a pass-rate or score-improvement guarantee for children who complete the programme as directed would address the high-stakes, outcome-focused mindset of 11+ parents.
