- **Coordinates & reflections** (Q25, Q38, Q42) — coordinate geometry not a standalone topic
- **Surface area** (Q39) — app covers volume but not surface area of prisms
- **Calendar calculations** (Q34) — days of the week / date arithmetic not covered
