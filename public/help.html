Help & Support

Quick answers to common questions. If yours isn't here, get in touch below.

Get in touch

Email hello@prepstep.co.uk — we usually reply within one working day.

For urgent issues (e.g. account access), please put URGENT in the subject line.

Getting started

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign up" on the homepage. We'll ask for your email, your name, and your child's first name. We never ask for your child's surname, school, or date of birth.

Can I have more than one child on the same account?

Not currently. Each PrepStep account is for one child so we can keep their progress and weekly emails accurate. If you have multiple children preparing for the 11+, please create a separate account for each.

What's the difference between Daily Learning, Focused Learning, and Mock Tests?
  • Daily Learning — 10 mixed questions across topics. Best for everyday short practice.
  • Focused Learning — Pick one topic (e.g. Fractions). Includes a short lesson before the quiz so your child sees the method first.
  • Mock Tests — Timed exam-style papers. Best closer to the exam date once topic confidence is in place.

Account & access

I forgot my password

On the sign-in screen, click "Forgot password?" and follow the email link. Password resets are handled by our authentication provider Clerk and arrive within a minute.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Self-serve subscription management is coming. For now, email us at hello@prepstep.co.uk and we'll cancel within one working day. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account and all my child's data?

Sign in, go to Progress → Parent Dashboard, scroll to the bottom and click "Delete account & all data". This is permanent and cannot be undone — all quiz history, progress, and your child's profile will be removed immediately.

You can also email us if you'd prefer we handled it for you.

How do I download my child's data?

Sign in, go to Progress → Parent Dashboard, scroll to the bottom and click "Download all data". You'll get a JSON file with your child's full quiz history, lesson completions, and progress data — required by UK GDPR.

Emails & notifications

I'm not receiving weekly progress emails

Check that you're opted in: sign in, go to Progress → Parent Dashboard → Data & Privacy and toggle "Weekly progress emails" on. Emails are sent every Sunday evening (UK time).

If you're opted in but still not receiving them, check your spam folder and add hello@prepstep.co.uk to your contacts.

How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Either toggle "Weekly progress emails" off in the Parent Dashboard, or click the unsubscribe option in any email we send (most email clients show this above the email body).

Content & quality

I think a question or answer is wrong

Please email us with the topic name and the question text (or a screenshot). We review every report and fix issues within a few days. Your feedback genuinely improves the app for every other family.

Is this affiliated with GL Assessment?

No. PrepStep is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to GL Assessment in any way. Our questions are written to reflect GL Assessment patterns based on publicly available information about the exam format.

Will using PrepStep guarantee my child passes the 11+?

No. PrepStep is a practice tool — it cannot guarantee any particular exam outcome. We're built to help children become familiar with question types, build confidence, and identify weaker topics. Effort, time, and the teaching your child gets at school all matter too.

Privacy & safety

How is my child's data stored?

Securely on Cloudflare's infrastructure (UK/EU data centres). We collect only what's needed to run the service — your email, your name, your child's first name, and their learning progress. We do not collect your child's surname, school, date of birth, or location.

For full details see our Privacy Policy.

Is the AI Tutor safe for my child?

Yes. The AI Tutor is restricted to discussing the specific exam question your child is studying. It cannot ask for personal information, talk about other topics, or share your child's data. Conversations are not stored on our servers.

What's your DPIA and AADC compliance?

PrepStep is registered with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO Reg: ZC117769) and has a Data Protection Impact Assessment in place. We follow the Age Appropriate Design Code (Children's Code) — including high-privacy defaults, no commercial profiling, and no advertising.

Technical issues

The app shows "Something went wrong"

This usually clears with a page reload. Click the "Reload" button on the error screen, or hard-refresh (Ctrl+F5 on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).

If it keeps happening, please email us with: what you were doing, the device and browser, and the time it happened. We'll investigate.

The app is slow or laggy

PrepStep works best on a stable Wi-Fi connection. If your child uses an older device, closing other apps and tabs may help. We're continuously optimising performance — if you see specific slowness, please tell us.
