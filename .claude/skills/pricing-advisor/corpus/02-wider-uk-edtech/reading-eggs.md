## Overview

Reading Eggs (published by Blake eLearning, an Australian edtech company) is a structured phonics and reading programme for children aged 2–13. It is built around a progression from early phonics through to independent reading comprehension, delivered via animated lessons, decodable books, and games. The UK site (readingeggs.co.uk) serves the British market with GBP pricing. A companion maths programme, Mathseeds, is available as a bundle. Reading Eggs is primarily a home product but is also used in UK primary schools. It is notable in the UK market for its generous multi-child bundling: up to 4 children are included in a single subscription at no extra cost.

## Pricing tiers

| Plan | Monthly | Annual | Annual equivalent/month | Annual saving |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Reading only | £6.99/month | £54.99/year | £4.58/month | 34% |
| Reading + Maths | £9.99/month | £79.99/year | £6.67/month | 33% |

Both plans include up to **4 child accounts**.

## Free vs paid

- **30-day free trial** on all plans (full access, not a limited preview).
- **30-day money-back guarantee** applies even after the trial, meaning there is effectively a 60-day low-risk window for new subscribers.
- No permanent free tier; all content is paywalled after the trial period.
- Trial requires a payment method at signup; cancellation during the trial incurs no charge.

The 30-day trial is one of the longest free access windows in the UK edtech market and is a meaningful acquisition lever, particularly for parents who want to see genuine engagement before committing to an annual plan.

## Family / sibling pricing

Up to 4 children are included in a single subscription — no sibling add-ons, no tiered family plans, no additional cost. This is a flat-rate household model rather than a per-child model. For a family with 3 or 4 children aged 2–13, the effective per-child cost on the annual reading plan falls to £13.75/child/year — an exceptionally low unit cost. This makes Reading Eggs one of the most price-competitive options per child in the market.

## Schools licensing (if applicable)

Reading Eggs is available to schools under a separate school licensing model, but specific per-pupil school pricing is not published on the UK consumer site. Schools typically contact Blake eLearning directly or purchase via authorised UK resellers. School access includes teacher dashboards, class management, and curriculum alignment reporting.

## Unusual mechanics

- **Up to 4 children, flat price:** The most notable structural feature of Reading Eggs pricing. No other UK kids edtech product in this comparison set offers 4 child accounts at the base price with no upgrade required.
- **30-day trial + 30-day guarantee:** The combination of a long free trial and a post-trial guarantee is unusual and reduces perceived risk substantially at point of purchase.
- **692 lessons, 4,000+ books:** The breadth of content is emphasised in the pricing page itself, suggesting that content volume is a key purchase justification.
- **Annual savings clearly shown:** The pricing page explicitly calls out "34% savings" and "33% savings" on annual plans, presenting the annual plan as the obvious default.

## Applicability to UK 11+ app pricing

The flat 4-child household model at £54.99–£79.99/year sets a strong price-anchoring benchmark for multi-child families. A UK 11+ app targeting the same parent demographic will face a comparison to this price point; the 11+ product must justify a higher price through specificity (exam focus, GL/CEM alignment, regional customisation) rather than competing on breadth or volume of content.
