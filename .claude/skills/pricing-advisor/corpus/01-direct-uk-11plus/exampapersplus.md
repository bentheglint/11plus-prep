## Overview

ExamPapersPlus (EPP) is a pay-per-download resource provider targeting families preparing for grammar and independent school 11+ exams across England. It does not offer a subscription model — every purchase is a one-time transaction. The product range spans school-specific practice papers, video masterclasses, online tuition, creative writing marking services, and diagnostic tools. EPP's competitive positioning rests on specificity: resources are tailored to individual schools' exam formats, timing, and question structures, which is a meaningful differentiator for families targeting a specific grammar school. The brand claims to be "trusted by over 100,000 families" and is rated 4.9 by parents.

## Pricing tiers

EPP uses transactional pricing rather than subscription tiers. Typical price points (pre-discount):

| Product type | Typical price |
|---|---|
| Individual practice paper packs | ~£20 |
| School-specific preparation guides | £25–£59 |
| Small bundles | ~£156 |
| Large / comprehensive bundles | £272–£579 |
| Video masterclasses | Varies (part of bundles or standalone) |
| Online tuition | Separate pricing (not included in discount codes) |
| Creative writing marking | Separate pricing (not included in discount codes) |

Prices above are standard rates; promotional and seasonal discounts frequently reduce these significantly (see below).

## Free vs paid

No free trial. EPP offers **sample pages** on individual products (indicated by "Sample Available" badges), allowing parents to preview content quality and format before purchasing. Once a product is accessed after purchase, no refund is available.

## Family / sibling pricing

None observed. Purchases are per-resource rather than per-child, so a single bundle can theoretically serve multiple children preparing for the same school — though this is not marketed explicitly.

## Unusual mechanics

- **Discount codes:** EPP runs tiered discount codes regularly. The standing code **EPPDISCOUNT** gives 10% off orders over £70 and 20% off orders over £250. Seasonal codes (e.g. **SPRING**: 30% off for 36 hours) create urgency and drive bulk purchasing. Online tuition and writing marking are excluded from code discounts.
- **No-refund policy post-access:** Once digital content is accessed, the sale is final. This is standard for downloadable resources but worth noting given the relatively high per-unit cost.
- **School-specific content:** Rather than generic 11+ practice, EPP maps its resources to named schools and their documented exam formats — a high-intent, high-specificity product.
- **Trusted by 100,000+ families** claim and 4.9 parent rating are prominently featured as social proof.

## Positioning copy

- "Trusted by over 100,000 families" — headline social proof claim.
- "School-specific Exam Format, Timing & Structure" — core value proposition emphasising the specificity of the content.
- "Practice Makes Perfect" — brand strapline.
- "Walk in prepared and stand out" — aspirational outcome framing used on product pages.

## Applicability to UK 11+ app pricing

EPP demonstrates that parents will spend £150–500+ in a single transaction when the content is sufficiently school-specific and outcome-credible. A subscription app cannot replicate EPP's school-specificity model directly, but can borrow the framing: positioning practice as preparation for a named outcome (e.g. "Ready for the GL Assessment") rather than generic skill-building lifts perceived value significantly and justifies a higher monthly price.
