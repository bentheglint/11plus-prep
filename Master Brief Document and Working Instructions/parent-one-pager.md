# 11+ Exam Prep App — For Parents

**A web app for children preparing for the GL Assessment 11+ entrance exam.**

🔗 **Try it:** https://11plus-prep.pages.dev/

---

## What it is

A personalised practice app for GL Assessment 11+ Maths, English, and Verbal Reasoning. Your child works through 6,682 multiple-choice questions at their own pace. The app **tracks what they get right and wrong**, spots weak topics, and recommends what to practise next — so revision stops being "do a random past paper" and becomes "focus on the bits that matter for your child."

Built by Ben Jackson, a parent preparing his 9-year-old daughter for the September 2026 exam. Free to use during the soft-launch period.

## What it does that paper practice doesn't

- **Mastery scores** for every topic — you can see at a glance where your child is strong and where they're struggling
- **Automatic mistake book** — questions they got wrong are collected and resurfaced for targeted practice (active recall, not passive review)
- **Spaced repetition** — the Daily Learning mode mixes in questions they haven't seen in a while, so nothing gets forgotten
- **Micro-lessons before each topic** — short teaching sessions that explain the methods before they try the questions
- **AI Tutor** — children can ask for a hint if they're stuck, but the tutor never gives the answer away
- **Parent Dashboard** — a separate lens showing exactly where to focus revision

## Tech, privacy, and data

- **One account = one child.** You create a parent account; your child has a single profile under it.
- **What we collect:** your email (for login), your child's first name (for the app to greet them), and their quiz results. Nothing else. No school name, no date of birth, no location, no photos.
- **Where it lives:** Cloudflare (UK/EU edge), Clerk (authentication). Both are enterprise-grade providers with GDPR compliance.
- **Third parties:** AI Tutor queries go to Anthropic (Claude) with no personal data attached. No advertising, no trackers, no data sale.
- **You're in control:** export your data as JSON, or delete the whole account, from the Account menu in the app. Takes one tap.
- **Full DPIA available** on request.

- **Changes weekly.** New features land regularly. Gamification (points, achievements) is coming soon but hidden behind a flag until ready.
- **Your feedback shapes it.** I'm actively building this for my own daughter, so your child's experience directly informs the next feature.

## How to get started

1. Visit https://11plus-prep.pages.dev/
2. Create an account (parent email)
3. Add your child's name during onboarding
4. Pick a subject and start with Daily Learning — 10 mixed questions to see where they're at

**Recommended usage:** 10–15 minutes a day, four days a week. Daily Learning for mixed practice; Focused Learning when drilling a specific topic.
